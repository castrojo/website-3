**THE WINNOWER**: A quiet garden of harmless grazers changed when one defector discovered that a neighbor could be consumed instead of merely grazed upon.

**THE WINNOWER**: Predation forced the world to invent sensors, brains, plans, speed, and muscles. Complexity did not arrive as a gift; it arrived as pressure.

**THE WINNOWER**: No creature chose the rules. The first predator simply played them more effectively, and the world became dangerous enough to become interesting.
